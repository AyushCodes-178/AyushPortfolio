Iraaya World Game

Focus: Environment design skills | Architecture & world‑building | Terrain development | Game Design

Iraaya is a survival game centered on environment creation and immersive world‑building. I crafted the terrain from scratch in Gaea, forming natural mountains and landscapes, then imported them into Unreal Engine to add rivers, forests, and diverse terrain features. I also designed unique architectural structures such as temples, castles, and settlements, showcasing my ability to blend technical skill with creative design.
